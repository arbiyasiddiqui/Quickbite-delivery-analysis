# QuickBite Delivery – Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 1. Project Overview

The QuickBite Delivery Performance Analysis project focuses on evaluating delivery operations and customer experience for a food delivery platform. The goal of this project was to analyze delivery data to identify operational inefficiencies such as delivery delays, partner performance variations, and customer satisfaction patterns. By analyzing this data, the objective was to generate insights that could help improve delivery efficiency and overall service quality.

## 2. Business Problem

The food delivery company was facing several operational challenges. Many orders were getting delayed, and management did not have clear visibility into which cities or delivery partners were responsible for these delays. Additionally, customer ratings were fluctuating and it was difficult to identify the factors affecting customer satisfaction. Without proper analysis, the company could not optimize delivery performance or improve the customer experience.

## 3. Data Source

The dataset used for this project contains delivery order information including order details, delivery partner performance, city information, customer ratings, and payment modes. The data was prepared and organized in Google Sheets before importing it into Power BI for further analysis.

## 4. Data Preparation

Before building the dashboard, the dataset was reviewed and structured properly. Data types were verified, unnecessary columns were removed, and column names were standardized to ensure consistency. The cleaned dataset was then loaded into Power BI for analysis.

## 5. Tools Used

* Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard development
* Google Sheets – Data preparation and organization
* Data Analysis Techniques – KPI analysis, trend analysis, and performance evaluation

## 6. Key KPIs Created

Several key performance indicators were created to monitor delivery performance:

* Total Orders – Total number of delivery orders processed
* Average Delivery Delay – Average delay in order delivery
* Average Customer Rating – Overall customer satisfaction level
* Total Delivery Partners – Number of delivery partners involved
* Total Cities Covered – Number of cities where services are available

These KPIs help management quickly understand the overall performance of delivery operations.

## 7. Dashboard Analysis

The Power BI dashboard includes multiple visualizations to analyze delivery performance from different perspectives:

City-wise Order Distribution – Shows which cities generate the highest number of orders.

Delivery Delay Analysis – Helps identify cities or delivery partners where delays occur more frequently.

Customer Rating Distribution – Shows how customers rate their delivery experience.

Payment Mode Analysis – Analyzes the distribution of different payment methods used by customers.

Partner Performance Analysis – Helps identify high-performing and low-performing delivery partners.

These visualizations allow management to quickly identify patterns and operational challenges.

## 8. Key Insights

The analysis revealed several important insights:

Some cities experienced higher delivery delays compared to others.

Certain delivery partners consistently handled more orders and maintained better customer ratings.

Customer satisfaction was closely linked to delivery speed and service efficiency.

Payment preferences showed that digital payments were commonly used by customers.

These insights help management understand operational performance more clearly.

## 9. Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, several recommendations can be made:

Improve delivery partner allocation in cities with frequent delays.

Provide training or performance monitoring for underperforming delivery partners.

Optimize delivery routes to reduce delays.

Encourage digital payment methods for faster transactions.

These steps can help improve operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

## 10. Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analysis and interactive dashboards can help businesses monitor operational performance and identify areas for improvement. The Power BI dashboard provides a clear and interactive view of delivery operations, enabling management to make data-driven decisions that improve service quality and customer experience.
